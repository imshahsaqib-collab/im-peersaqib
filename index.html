What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of smart devices connected to the internet, capable of collecting and exchanging real-time data.

Where IoT is used?

  • Smart homes (lights, cameras, climate control)
  • Wearables (smartwatches, fitness trackers)
  • Industrial automation & monitoring
  • Vehicle monitoring & GPS
  • Smart farming and agriculture

Why IoT matters?

IoT makes systems automatic, efficient, and real-time by using sensors, microcontrollers (ESP32/Arduino) and cloud services to monitor & control devices remotely.

Typical IoT Stack:

  • Sensors ‚Üí Microcontroller (ESP32/Arduino) ‚Üí WiFi / MQTT ‚Üí Cloud Dashboard
